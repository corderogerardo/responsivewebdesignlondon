How to Make No Bake Key Lime Pie

    Finished?
  1. Mix the Graham cracker crumbs and butter.
  2. Form into pie shell.
  3. Blend the cream cheese until you can move it easily with a spatula.p
  4. Add sweetened condensed milk until it is blended with cream cheese.
  5. Add lime juice and blend only until it is all combined (about 2 to 3 minutes). Hint: you don't want bubbles or it won't set.
  6. Pour cream mixture into pie shell.
  7. Refrigerate for approximately 5 hours.
  8. Serve cold.
  9. Finished.
